Our Journey

From a single-roaster startup to a community staple — our story is one of craft and care.

Roots & Roast

We opened in 2024 with a simple philosophy: source responsibly, roast thoughtfully, and serve with warmth. Our beans are carefully sourced from small farms and roasted in small batches to bring out natural sweetness and depth.

Community

Local partnerships and events matter — from latte art nights to pop-up tastings, we aim to put people first. Every cup supports a network of growers and baristas who believe in great coffee.

Visit Us

Stop by for a cup and see the process — you can even watch a roast in action on select Saturdays.
